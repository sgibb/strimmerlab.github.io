Korbinian Strimmer selected to list "Highly Cited Researchers 2016":

Please visit the "Highly Cited Researchers" webpage.

In November 2016 Korbinian Strimmer has been selected to the list Highly Cited Researchers 2016 published by Clarivate Analytics (formerly Thomson Reuters) which includes approximately 3000 scientists in 22 different areas of research. The compilation is based on the top 1% ranking publications from 2004 to 2014.
