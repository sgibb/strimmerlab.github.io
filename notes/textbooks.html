Online Textbooks in Statistics and Machine Learning

(back to index)

 

Some interesting statistics books with full text freely available online:

  • Core Statistics (printer-friendly version) by Simon Wood (2014)
  • An Introduction to Statistical Learning with Applications in R by Gareth James, Daniela Witten, Trevor Hastie and Robert Tibshirani (2013)
  • Advanced Data Analysis from an Elementary Point of View by Cosma Rohilla Shalizi (2013).
  • Bayesian Reasoning and Machine Learning by David Barber (2012).
  • Principles of Uncertainty by Joseph Kadane (2011).
  • Draft version of Large-Scale Inference by Bradley Efron (2010)
  • Applied Regression by Kathryn Roeder (2010)
  • The Elements of Statistical Learning (2nd edition) by Trevor Hastie, Robert Tibshirani, and Jerome Friedman (2009)
  • The Bayesian Choice (2nd edition) by Christian Robert (2007)
  • Introduction to Statistical Thought by Michael L. Lavine (2006)
  • Understanding Uncertainty by Dennis Lindley (2006)
  • Gaussian Processes for Machine Learning by Carl Edward Rasmussen and Christopher K. I. Williams (2006).
  • Information Theory, Inference, and Learning Algorithms by David J. C. MacKay (2003).
  • Fragmentary version of Probability Theory: The Logic of Science by Edwin T. Jaynes (1994).
