Korbinian Strimmer selected to list "Highly Cited Researchers 2014":

Download the Thomson Reuters publication "The World's Most Influential Scientific Minds 2014" (pdf)

In June 2014 Korbinian Strimmer has been selected to the list Highly Cited Researchers 2014 published by Thomson Reuters which includes approximately 3000 researchers in 21 different areas of research. The compilation is based on the top 1% ranking publications from 2002 to 2012 and is a revision of an earlier list dating back to 2001.

---


(click image for more information about Korbinian)

I am delighted and honored to have been selected to the Thomson Reuters list "Highly Cited Researchers 2014" in the field of Computer Science!

Most of the research in the past decade has been joint work with doctoral students and research associates. Without their achievement this fantastic award would have not been possible! Thank you!

Korbinian Strimmer
Leipzig, June 2014
