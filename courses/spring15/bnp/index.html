Synopsis:

In this reading seminar we will discuss the theory and applications of nonparametric Bayes methodology. As a starting point we will use the book "Bayesian Nonparametrics" edited by Nils Lid Hjort, Chris Holmes, Peter Müller, and Stephen G. Walker. (2010, Cambridge University Press), but we will also read other papers and preprints as we see fit.

If would like to join in please add your name to this Doodle list!

Literature:

  • "Bayesian Nonparametrics" by Nils Lid Hjort, Chris Holmes, Peter Müller, and Stephen G. Walker. 2010. Cambridge University Press.
  • Gershman and Blei. 2012. A tutorial on Bayesian nonparametric models.
  • Yau and Holmes. 2011. Hierarchical Bayesian nonparametric mixture models for clustering with variable relevance determination.
  • Liverani et al. 2014. PReMiuM: An R Package for Profile Regression Mixture Models using Dirichlet Processes
