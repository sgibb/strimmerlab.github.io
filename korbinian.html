I am a Reader at Imperial College London. Together with my group and colleagues I work on problems in biomedical data science and develop corresponding statistical methods and computational tools, with special focus on high-dimensional data.

I have been selected by Thomson Reuters as "Highly Cited Researcher" in the field of computer science in the years 2015 and 2014, taking into account publications from 2002 to 2013.
