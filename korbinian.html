I am a Reader in Biostatistics and Computational Biology at Imperial College London. My main research interest is developing effective computational tools and statistical methodology for high-dimensional data analysis, with focus on life sciences.

I am listed among Highly Cited Researchers 2014 in the field of "Computer Science", taking into account publications from 2002 to 2012 (more details).
